FAQ

Questions, answered.

Yes. WP Travel Machine ships its own styled templates and works with any standard WordPress theme. Every template can be overridden from your theme folder for full control.

Stripe and PayPal for online payments, plus manual bank transfer. Stripe is SCA / 3-D Secure ready (PaymentIntents) and PayPal is captured server-side — both verify the charge amount before confirming. Prices are always recalculated server-side, so totals can’t be tampered with from the browser.

The AI features are optional. To enable them, add a key from any supported provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, Gemini, OpenRouter, Ollama or a custom endpoint). Everything else works without it.
